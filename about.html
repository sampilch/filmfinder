About

Movies are more accessible than ever thanks to streaming services, but there are so many options! Scrolling through a category trying to find something to watch is time-consuming and you're not guaranteed to find a movie you're interested in.

That's where Film Finder comes in! This short quiz gets straight to the point and recommends you a movie to watch (and two similar options if you're not satisfied).

All 27 movies on this website were handpicked and chosen with love. Whether they are critically acclaimed or a cult classic, I personally recommend every film.

This quiz is by no means comprehensive, but I would love to add more questions and movies in the future!
