Eternal Sunshine of the Spotless Mind

Year: 2004 Runtime: 108 min Rating: R Directed by: Michel Gondry Rotten Tomatoes Score: 92%

After a painful breakup, Clementine undergoes a procedure to erase memories of her former boyfriend Joel from her mind. When Joel discovers that Clementine is going to extremes to forget their relationship, he undergoes the same procedure and slowly begins to forget the woman that he loved. The visually arresting film explores the intricacy of relationships and the pain of loss.
