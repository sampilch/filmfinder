Bottoms

Year: 2023 Runtime: 92 min Rating: R Directed by: Emma Seligman Rotten Tomatoes Score: 91%

This refreshingly unique raunchy comedy focuses on two girls, PJ and Josie, who start a fight club as a way to lose their virginities to cheerleaders. Their bizarre plan works. The fight club gains traction and soon the most popular girls in school are beating each other up in the name of self-defense. But PJ and Josie find themselves in over their heads and in need of a way out before their plan is exposed.
